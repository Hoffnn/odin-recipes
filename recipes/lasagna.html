Lasagna Recipe

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait.

The ingredients are:

  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg
  • Spice and seasonings

Follow the steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the Lasagna in the following order
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Noodles
  7. Ricotta mixture
  8. Mozzarella
  9. Meat sauce
  10. Parmesean cheese
  11. Repeat the layers
  12. Cover with foil and bake. Let it rest before serving.
